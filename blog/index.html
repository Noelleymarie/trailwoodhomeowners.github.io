Trailwood HOA Board of Directors Blog

Thanks for looking at our weblog! This blog is used to share information written by members of the Board of Directors and HOA members in order to communicate things of a less ephemeral nature than we can share on Facebook, where things are lost quickly.

If something becomes important enough or sticky enough, we’ll probably add it to a more permanent part of the website.

If you’d like to request a blog topic, or even have a blog post you’d like to see, please write to any of our board members.
